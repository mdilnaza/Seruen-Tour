About Akmola Region

If you happen to visit the capital of Kazakhstan - Nur-Sultan, at least for a few days, be sure to explore its surroundings.
Only in a few hours’ drive, you can visit the northernmost habitat of pink flamingos or enjoy trekking or rock-climbing among the beautiful lakes
and mountains covered with pine-tree forests, while staying at one of the spa hotels.

Attractions In Akmola

Burabay National Park

Surrounded by many legends and stories, Burabay has at least 14 lakes, surrounded by mountains and evergreen forest. The best view of the lakes is open from the top of Mount Bolektau, which is very easy to climb. In addition to trekking, climbing, swimming in lakes, boating, and other outdoor activities, you can visit a tourist center with a yurt village to learn more about the culture of nomads, or the Botai-Burabay Museum, which demonstrates dwellings of the tribes, who first domesticated horses about 6000 years ago.

Korgaljyn Nature Reserve

Birdwatching tours are very popular in the reserve due to the fact that the northernmost flamingo population nests here. The best time to observe the birds is in the spring – the nesting season – and in the autumn when they gather in large flocks to migrate to warmer climes. In summer, flamingos are scattered across the lakes and you can see them, but in smaller numbers.

ALZHIR Museum

The Akmola Concentration Camp for Wives of Traitors to the Motherland is a notorious place where more than 18 thousand unfortunate wives of the so-called "public enemies" were imprisoned. They were considered “socially dangerous and capable of anti-Soviet actions”. There is the famous “Arch of Sorrow”, one should bow one's head while passing through it.

How to get There
  • By Plane

    Air Astana is the national air carrier of Kazakhstan, based in Almaty.
    It operates regular international and domestic flights on 64 routes from its main hub, Almaty International Airport,
    and from a secondary hub, Nursultan Nazarbayev International Airport.

  • By Train

    If you are already in another city of Kazakhstan, you can get to Almaty by train.
    You will arrive in Almaty 1, or to Almaty 2,
    it's closer to downtown from which you can get to any part.

  • By Bus

    There are plenty of bus routes for tourists to go anywhere for cheap price in the city of Almaty.
    Today, fare payment is available with a transport card, as well as by means of a QR code.
    There are the following mobile applications for this: Transcard, Kaspi.kz,
    SMSBUS, Homebank, My Activ, My Beeline, Senim, Rakhmet, Astra Bus, Plus24 (Kassa24), Qiwi and OYBY

  • On foot

    A walk allows you to immerse yourself in the pleasure of traveling from one point to another.
    You will get to know the city you are visiting better, besides, it's possible to talk with the locals to get acquainted with Kazakh culture, see amazing artistic architecture and just enjoy!
